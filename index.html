About Me

My paintings are recognized for their impressionistic style. It’s all about the focus, the purpose, the one strong statement. My color palette is limited to single subjects yet they make the vivid colors sing in harmony...

I often ask myself: why bother? But this is what we call passion. I wouldn’t call myself a ‘great artist’, rather a being who reflects emotion on canvas...

People ask: “how do you create these paintings?” I believe art is for everyone willing to try...

Canvas by Heart promotes cultural expression and innovation—encouraging art as dialogue in our fast world.
